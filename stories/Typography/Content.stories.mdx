<h2>Content</h2>

<p>
  The content tag is used to render standard typography with paragraph blocks
  styled using GDS styling.
</p>

<p>
  You can view the full implementation details on the{' '}
  <a
    href="https://design-system.service.gov.uk/styles/typography/"
    target="_blank"
    rel="noreferrer"
  >
    Typography
  </a>{' '}
  section of the GDS website.
</p>

<h2>Options</h2>

<p>
  The content block renders in a p tag as standard but can be overridden to
  render as a pre tag.
</p>
